Privacy Policy of Stuffie Wishes
June 21th, 2020.

Stuffie Wishes is an Instant Game developed by Team Tallarin.
The game is hosted by Facebook, Inc. and uses the tools provided by them in order to make a fun experience for everyone.

We don't collect nor store any type of data.
The only data used by our app (Stuffie Wishes) is your name and your profile picture, both provided by Facebook and showed to you on the main screen of the game.
